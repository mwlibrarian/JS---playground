How to figure your wage, after taxes.

  1. First enter your hourly wage (how much you get paid per hour).

  2. Next enter the number of hours that you work per week, not counting overtime.

  3. Than enter your state tax rate in decimal form.

  4. Next, enter your federal tax rate in decimal form.

  5. Finally, click on this last box to see your final wage.

  6. Now your done, so stop.
