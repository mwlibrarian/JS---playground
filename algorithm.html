How to figure your wage, after taxes.

  1. First enter your hourly wage (how much you get paid per hour).

  2. Next enter the number of hours that you work per week, not counting overtime.

  3. Click on the next box to find your gross weekly wage (wage before taxes).

  4. Next, type your gross wage into the following box.

  5. Then type your rate of federal tax, in decimal form, in the next box.
  6. Click on the next box to find your fed tax owed at the given rate of your federal tax

  7. Next, again type your gross wage into the following box.

  8. Then type your rate of state tax, in decimal form, in the next box.
  9. Click on the next box to find your state tax owed at the given rate of your state tax

  10. Next, again type your gross wage into the following box.

  11. Click on the last box to view your final wage after taxes
  12. Now your done, so stop.
